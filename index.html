Grass Network ($GRASS) Deep Due Diligence Report

Date: June 2026
Ticker: GRASS
Sector: DePIN / AI Data Infrastructure / Bandwidth Marketplace
Chain: Solana

Executive Summary

Grass is one of the few crypto projects with evidence of real commercial activity. Unlike most DePIN projects that primarily sell a token narrative, Grass sells access to large-scale public web datasets collected through a distributed network of residential internet connections.

Its investment case depends on a single question: Can Grass become a major supplier of AI training data?

If yes, GRASS may become one of the strongest revenue-backed crypto assets in the AI sector. If not, it risks becoming another incentive-driven network whose token value depends primarily on speculation.

1. What Does Grass Actually Do?

Grass allows users to share unused internet bandwidth. The network routes requests through residential IPs and gathers publicly available web data. That data is processed and sold primarily to AI companies.

Grass is effectively building a decentralized data acquisition layer for AI.

Business flow:

  1. Users run Grass node/browser extension/app.
  2. Users contribute bandwidth.
  3. Grass gathers public web data.
  4. Data is structured and packaged.
  5. AI companies purchase datasets.
  6. Revenue funds operations and ecosystem growth.

12. Bull Case

  • Real revenue exists.
  • Revenue appears to be growing rapidly.
  • AI data market is expanding.
  • Massive user network already built.
  • One of the largest Solana airdrops ever.
  • Buybacks funded from revenue.
  • Strong AI narrative.

13. Bear Case

  • Large future token unlocks.
  • Revenue heavily dependent on AI demand.
  • No audited financial statements.
  • High holder concentration.
  • Community frustration around delayed rewards.
  • Business customer concentration risk.
  • Potential regulatory scrutiny around residential proxy networks.

14. Valuation View

GRASS is not a traditional equity and holders do not own company cash flows. However, revenue is useful as a proxy for network adoption.

If revenue run-rate remains around $50M+ annually and continues growing, GRASS may deserve premium valuation versus typical DePIN projects.

If revenue stalls while unlocks continue, token performance may suffer regardless of product success.
