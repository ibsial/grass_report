Executive Summary

Grass is one of the few crypto projects with publicly disclosed commercial revenue. The investment debate has shifted from “does the business work?” to “how much value accrues to token holders?”

Strength: Real revenue, real customers, AI demand.

Risk: Future unlocks, concentration, weak direct value capture.

Business Model

Users → Bandwidth → Public Web Data → Structured Datasets → AI Companies → Revenue

Grass collects public web data through residential bandwidth and sells processed datasets to AI companies.
